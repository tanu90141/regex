What is a Flower? A flower is the bloom or blossom of a plant. The flower is the part of a plant that produces seeds, which in time become other flowers. This is thanks to a process called pollination that is carried out either by insects or the wind. Every type of flower is different, in colour, smell, shape and size. Flowers need food and sunlight to survive, as they make their own food/energy through photosynthesis.
